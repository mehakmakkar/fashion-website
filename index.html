My name is Mehak Makkar. I am an upcoming fashion designer and model. I grew up in India and moved to Canada for my undergraduation. As a child, I was always artistic and a fitness enthusiast. I loved dressing up my dolls, myself, performing solo and duo dance performances and performed competitive yoga. I am extremely passionate about becoming a globally renowned fashion designer and launching my personal brand.
